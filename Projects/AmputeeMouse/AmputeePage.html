Brainstorming

This was the first Hackathon any of us had been to, and the first time we had worked together. We had no expectations, no valuable programming skills, and most evidently no ideas coming into the event. After the beginning introductions from the event staff, we set off to figure out some tangible idea that we could accomplish in the 36 hour timeframe. The one thing that impacted us from their speeches we're to aim to improve the community in one way or another. After learning about Jack's(one of the teammates) cousin who was missing their hand from a car accident, we begin looking at ways we could improve their quality of life. Using a mouse is one of those

Our team discovering the pain of sleep deprivation and frustration for the first time. We ran into many problems when we first attempted to use an Arduino Uno. We ran into problems reading the accelerometer data for some reason and switched over to the hackathon supplied Intel Edision.
